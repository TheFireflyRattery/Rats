My rattery is still active.

I usually have litters every couple of months. However I usually have a waiting list so I can not guarantee that babies will always be immediately available. I always recommend sending in an application, getting approved, and sending in a deposit so you can secure your place on the waiting list.

I do not typically breed in the summer. (My last litter is generally ready around June and then I do not pair ladies again until September.)

Pairing may take a few weeks or more to be successful. (Rats naturally breed less in the cold months so sometimes it takes longer.) Once successful gestation is around 21 days and then babies will be ready to go home about six weeks after they are born.

Please check the Facebook page for current litter updates. All litter updates and photos get posted to the Facebook page.
