The Firefly Rattery - Specializing in breeding healthy, happy, and well socialized dumbo rats. Each baby is loved and treated like my own pet.


Established: June 2005.

Location: Western Massachusetts.


Ear Type: Dumbo only.
Fur Type: Standard (smooth) fur and Rex (curly) fur.
Colors: Agouti, Blue Agouti, Blue, Black, Fawn (rarely), and White.
Pattern: Berkshire, Irish Berkshire, Variegated Berkshire, Opossum, Hooded, Variegated Hooded, Blaze (wedge blaze and crooked blaze), BEW (black eyed white), Self (one solid color, no white), Patch Eye, Dalmatian, and head spot.

Welcome to my Rattery website. This is the place where you can find information on my rats, current and past litters, view my rat logs, and so much more. If you have any questions feel free to email me and ask.


Want to adopt a rat but don't see any available? Just email. I make up waiting lists and keep people updated on when a litter will be planned and born.
